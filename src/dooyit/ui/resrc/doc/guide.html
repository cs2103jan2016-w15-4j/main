Supported DateTime formats

Date Examples Interpretation
17 Feb 2016, feb 17 2016, 17/2/2016, 17/02/2016 17 Feb 2016
17 feb, feb 17, 17/2, 17/02 17 Feb of the current year if it has not passed, otherwise 17 Feb of next year
feb 15 Feb of the current year if it has not passed, otherwise 15 Feb of next year
this mon, mon The Monday that is less than a week away
next mon The Monday that is more than a week away
tdy, today Today
tmr, tomorrow Tomorrow
next week 7 days from the current date
3 days 3 days from the current date
2 wk, 2 week, 2 weeks 2 weeks from the current date

Time Examples Interpretation
10.30pm, 10.30 pm, 10:30pm, 10:30 pm, 22:30 10.30pm
10.30am, 10.30 am, 10:30am, 10:30 am, 10:30 10.30am
10am, 10 am, 10:00 10.00am
10pm, 10 pm, 22:00 10.00pm

Acceptable DateTime formats Examples
<Date> <Time> tmr 10pm
<Time> <Date> 10.30am fri
<Time> 7pm
<Date> next week
